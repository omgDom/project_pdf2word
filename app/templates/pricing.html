Frequently asked questions

What is SimpleDoc?

SimpleDoc is a powerful document conversion platform that transforms your files between different formats while preserving layout and formatting. Whether you need to convert PDFs to Word, Excel to PDF, or handle other document formats, our tool makes it simple and efficient.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, SimpleDoc offers a 2 week trial for its Premium plan where you can test advanced functionality. You can also take advantage of our free plan to edit and sign documents online for free.

What formats can I convert?

We support all major document formats including PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and images. Premium users get access to additional formats like CAD files, vector graphics, and more.

How does the daily limit work?

Free users can convert up to 5 documents per day. Once you reach your limit, you'll need to wait until the next day or upgrade to Premium for unlimited conversions.

Is my data secure?

All files are encrypted in transit and at rest. We automatically delete converted files after 24 hours and never store or share your data with third parties. You can also enable Privacy Mode for additional security.

What happens after I upgrade?

You'll immediately get access to all Premium features including unlimited conversions, priority processing, and advanced file formats. You can cancel or change your plan at any time.

Where can I ask more questions?

You're welcome to join our forum and share your thoughts! If you prefer a private conversation, feel free to email us directly.
